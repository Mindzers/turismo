Testimonials

“Pedra Bonita is characterized by being an adventure trail but much easier, It's simple, you don't need a guide, and you can see the sunset. I really recommend doing it, it's another very nice point of view.”

— Sofia Diaz, Traveler,Arg

“It's a super fun trilha, everyone wants to have the long-awaited photo but I recommend doing it at dawn to see the sunrise, it's the best time because you don't have to stand in line to take the photo. I classify it as easy, but it takes a long time to get there, you need a guide because it is far and after the descent you will need to rest without a doubt.”

— Manuel Murillo, Traveler,Colo

“This is the main Trilha in Rio de Janeiro, intermediate level. If you like adventure you will love this! It is recommended to be accompanied by someone who already knows the way to avoid any inconvenience. It is necessary to bring water and some food for after the climb, and a lot of patience because the view is beautiful. The guide was excellent, then we went to a restaurant in the middle of the Favela with a beautiful view, we ended up going down through the community, an unforgettable experience.”

— Sofia Diaz, Traveler,Arg
